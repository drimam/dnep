Import Duty and Taxes FAQ

What is import duty?

Import duty is a tax collected on imports and some exports by a country's customs authorities. A good's value will usually dictate the import duty.

Can I avoid paying import duty in Nepal?

Certain items may be exempt from import duty. However, the majority of goods imported to Nepal are subject to import duty.

What happens if I don’t pay the duty or tax?

Failure to pay the required duties or taxes may result in your shipment being held by customs or returned to the sender.
